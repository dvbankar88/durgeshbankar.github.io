Early Life and Education

Durgesh Vishnu Bankar was born on March 15, 1985, in Maharashtra, India. From an early age, he showed entrepreneurial spirit and a deep commitment to helping others in his community. He completed his Bachelor of Commerce from the University of Pune in 2007, which laid the foundation for his future business ventures.

Education

Bachelor of Commerce - University of Pune (2007)

Business Career

Mr. Bankar has established himself as a successful entrepreneur with multiple business ventures across diverse industries including real estate development, digital services, agricultural trading, and manufacturing. He started his first business venture in 2010 and has since built a diverse portfolio of successful companies.

Current Businesses

  • SN Properties Group - Real estate development and property management
  • SN Online Center (CSC Center) - Digital services including ITR, GST, and government services
  • Vitthala Agritrade Private Limited - Agricultural trading specializing in maize and soybean
  • Ayneer Water Bottle Plant - Upcoming manufacturing venture for packaged drinking water

Social Work and Philanthropy

Beyond his business achievements, Mr. Bankar is deeply committed to social welfare through SN Group Foundation - a non-profit organization focused on community development and helping those in need. He has helped over 1000 people through various welfare programs.

Social Initiatives

  • SN Group Foundation - Non-profit organization for community development
  • Educational support programs for underprivileged children
  • Healthcare initiatives in rural areas
  • Disaster relief and emergency support
  • Poverty alleviation programs

His mission is to create sustainable business solutions that not only generate profit but also contribute to the betterment of society. He believes in the power of entrepreneurship to drive positive social change and is committed to using his business success to help others.
