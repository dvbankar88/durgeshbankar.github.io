Biography

Durgesh Bankar is a prominent businessman, entrepreneur, and social worker from Maharashtra, India. Born with a vision to create sustainable business models that benefit both the economy and society, he has established himself as a respected figure in multiple sectors including real estate, digital services, social welfare, and manufacturing.

Early Life & Education

Growing up in Maharashtra, Durgesh developed an early interest in business and community service. His educational background laid the foundation for his entrepreneurial journey, combining academic knowledge with practical business acumen.

Professional Journey

Durgesh's professional journey began with a clear vision of "business with purpose." He believes that successful enterprises should not only generate profit but also contribute meaningfully to society. This philosophy has guided all his business ventures and social initiatives.

Business Philosophy

His approach to business is characterized by innovation, ethical practices, and community engagement. He actively seeks opportunities to create employment, support local communities, and contribute to economic development while maintaining the highest standards of business ethics.

Social Commitment

Beyond business success, Durgesh is deeply committed to social causes. Through the SN Group Foundation, he has initiated various programs focused on education, community development, and social welfare. His work in this area reflects his belief that successful individuals have a responsibility to give back to society.
